About

Tracy Polland

I founded RGC Technology Limited to bring senior-level IT leadership to organisations that need it — on the terms that work for them. After more than 25 years leading IT across complex global businesses, I understand what good technology leadership looks like at every level of an organisation, from the server room to the boardroom.

My career spans ERP and enterprise systems, IT infrastructure and end user services, M&A integration, and multi-geography operations. I've led SAP and Oracle EBS programmes across the Americas, Europe, and Asia Pacific, managed global infrastructure operations achieving 100% uptime over six years, and successfully completed a full TSA exit nine months ahead of schedule with no loss of business.

I've built and led high-performing global teams — both in-house and with major MSPs including TCS, NTT, and CapGemini — across industries including aerospace, manufacturing, electronics, healthcare, and commercial services.

I bring a direct, pragmatic approach to every engagement. I don't over-engineer solutions or create dependency — I work to leave organisations more capable than I found them.

The name RGC carries a meaning that matters to me: the initials of my three children. It's a reminder that everything I do professionally is grounded in something real.
